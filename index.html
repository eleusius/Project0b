This website is a very rough attempt to describe an idea that has haunted me for most of my adult life. An attempt to describe a Grand Unified Theory of Everything which unites physics, religion, and psychology into a single worldview based on the principle of 3 + 1. For example, beginning, middle, and end, and the space before and after, as in the syllable OM in Hinduism which consists of the three seed syllables A, OH, and HUM. Another example would be the four fundamental particles, with the neutrino being the +1 (a seemingly irrelevant particle whose function is unknown); or in ancient times the four elements earth, air, water, and fire, with fire being the “one of these things just doesn’t belong.”
